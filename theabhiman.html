Born from the island and built for the world. The Abhiman — meaning "pride" in Sinhala — is a statement of intent: a South Asian artist unafraid to occupy the largest stages on earth.

The chrome mask is not a disguise — it is the truest reflection of an artist in transformation. What lies beneath is the menace. What emerges is the saint.
